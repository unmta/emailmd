# Your Weekly Update :newspaper:

Hi there! Here's what happened this week at Acme.

## New Features :sparkles:

We shipped **three major features** this week that our users have been asking for:

- **Dark mode** — finally! Toggle it in your settings.
- **Bulk export** — download all your data as CSV or JSON.
- **Team dashboards** — see your team's activity at a glance.

> "The new dark mode is gorgeous. I can't believe it took this long!" — *a happy user*

## Getting Started with Teams

If you're on a Pro plan, team dashboards are already enabled. Here's how to get started:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Invite your teammates via email
3. Choose which metrics to display
4. Share the dashboard link with your org
